Aurora allows teams to celebrate progress and share the joy of getting stuff done. Aurora helps teams overcome roadblocks and inspires individuals to get unstuck, by pushing progress through gamified time management.

About

Our remote work landscape makes it tough to feel connected to what other people on your team are doing.

It is hard for team members to know that their work is contributing to collective progress and that their work is appreciated. Gap in communication leads to an abundance of emails and meetings that doesn’t always generate productive work

Time management activities can take a lot of time and distract from productivity, causing team members to experience burnout.

What if time management and team connection could be embedded into the work itself?

“Work just feels fun on Aurora because I can see that we’re all making progress as a team in the moment. I don’t have to send a million emails with granular tasks, instead I can see how much work is getting done. If I need to change my pace, I love switching to Aurora Pomodoro.”

Sarah Wilkins, Project Manager

Features.

Our Core Features

We embed productivitiy tools into our features that help you work your best, without the stress.

Suggestive Task Switching

Keep your progress going with Suggested Task Switching. Aurora automatically recommends times when switching between tasks can help prevent cognitive fatigue and burnout.

Pomodoro Rounds

Aurora builds in structured mental/physical breaks to prevent mental and physical fatigue. These are broken into 25 minute working sessions with 5 minute breaks.

Peer Accountability/ Momentum

Peers can work together and keep everyone inspired by collective progress. The team will not only hold each other accountable for getting work done, but also pursing wellbeing activities.
